Girls4Impact

An International Collaboration.

This was an 10 week long computer science collaboration program between ACM-DBIT Student Chapter and Girls4Impact an International orgainzation aiming to help young women in India explore their passion for engineering! This program was for female school students, in hopes to improve gender gap in engineering and tech industry.

Shantighar, Andheri

Official Website

Shantighar is a Shelter home for battered women and children who are in crisis. ACM-DBIT Student Chapter took the initiative and developed a website for Shantighar to further increase their digital outreach to the women and childern in crisis.
